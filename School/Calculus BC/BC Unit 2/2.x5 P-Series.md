* Note that you can move the constant in the numerator out and in front of the summation like how you would do that in an integral. 

* Do not get confused. The power in the denominator can not be a variable -- it has to be a finite number in order for P-Series to apply. (n$^Ï€$ and n$^e$ are still fair game). The "base" has to be n.
